With a background in user experience, system, and service Design, Hartmann works on transdisciplinary problems often surrounding the sustainable and social transformation of cities. As part of the European Design Studies master’s program, Hartmann studied at Köln International School of Design, ENSCI–Les Ateliers in Paris, and Aalto University in Helsinki.
